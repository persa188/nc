Who are we?

Precision Development (PD) believes that well planned and tactical deployments of caffeine is the secret to perfection. That is, of course, when added to our team of highly trained developers and aspiring software engineers that have worked on projects at the ever-so crunchy University of Toronto Scarborough, CIBC, Wind River Systems, ShawCor, CaseWare, and more. This diverse skillset and collective addiction to caffeinated beverages is why we've decided to aim for the number one spot at the CSCC01 Finale and eventually saving the world!
